About Me

Here you'll find information about my background, interests, and journey.

Hi, I'm Noor Shamasneh. My interest in web development started when I was learning to code for fun, and I became interested in making websites. Over time, that interest grew, and now I focus on designing and building websites that are both functional and visually appealing.

My approach is to blend creativity with practical solutions. I enjoy working on projects where I can help create something useful, and I appreciate the challenge of making a site not only look good but also work smoothly for users. My experience includes both designing from scratch and improving existing websites, always with the goal of making them better for the people who use them.
